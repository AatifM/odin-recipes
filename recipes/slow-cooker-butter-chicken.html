Slow Cooker Butter Chicken

Description

It's easy to prep this butter chicken in the slow cooker in the morning to come home to the wonderful aroma of warm Indian spices. Serve with basmati rice and warm naan bread.

Ingredients

  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • Vegetable oil (2 tbsp)
  • Large skinless boneless chicken thighs (4)
  • Onion (1 diced)
  • Garlic cloves (3 minced)
  • Tomato paste (1 can/6 ounces)
  • Curry paste (1 tbsp)
  • Curry powder (2 teaspoons)
  • Tandoori masala (2 teaspoons)
  • Garam masala (1 teaspoon)
  • Green cardamom pods (15)
  • Coconut milk (1 can/14 ounces)
  • Low-fat plain yogurt (1 cup)
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Heat butter and vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken, onion, and garlic. Cook and stir until onion has softened and turned translucent, about 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in tomato paste, curry paste, curry powder, tandoori masala, and garam masala until no lumps of tomato paste remain.
  3. Pour mixture into a slow cooker; stir in cardamom pods, coconut milk, and yogurt. Season with salt.
  4. Cook on High for 4 to 6 hours or Low for 6 to 8 hours, or until chicken is tender and sauce has reduced to the desired consistency. Remove and discard cardamom pods before serving.
