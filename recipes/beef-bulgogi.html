Beef Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi, sometimes known as Korean BBQ beef, is a dish of thinly sliced grilled steak that has been marinated in a sweet soy, sesame, and garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Steak (1 pound thinly sliced)
  • Soy Sauce (5 tbsp)
  • Sugar (2 and 1/2 tbsp)
  • Green Onion (1/4 Cup Chopped)
  • Garlic (2 tbsp)
  • Sesame Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Pepper (1/2 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
